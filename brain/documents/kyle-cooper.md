# Style Blueprint: Kyle Cooper

### 👁️ The Visual Signature
The mastermind behind the *Se7en* and *Meneace II Society* title sequences. Cooper’s style is tactile, aggressive, and deeply psychological. He treats typography not just as information, but as a living, breathing, deteriorating organism that drives narrative tension.

### 🎨 Color & Light
*   **Industrial Decay:** Muted, gritty color palettes dominated by sickly greens, rusted ambers, sepia, and deep industrial grays.
*   **Flickering Illumination:** High-contrast, erratic lighting that mimics old film projectors, flashlights, or xerox machines.

### 📐 Composition & Texture
*   **Extreme Macro Framing:** Super close-ups of textures—scratched emulsion, human skin, chopped notebooks, rusted metal—creating a sense of claustrophobia.
*   **Analog Layering:** Multi-layered compositions where handwritten text, mechanical typewriter fonts, and raw film grain clash violently over one another.

> **Creative Prompt Generator Idea:** "Apply a Kyle Cooper lens: Destabilize the typography. How can the text look like it was physically scratched, stamped, or decayed into the layout?"
