# Style Blueprint: Nick Knight

### 👁️ The Visual Signature
Nick Knight rejects passive photography. His work lives at the intersection of high fashion, technological experimentation, and fine art painting. He treats the digital camera as a digital paintbrush, often distorting realities to create hyper-expressive, futuristic imagery.

### 🎨 Color & Light
*   **High-Contrast Drama:** Aggressive highlights and deep, velvety blacks.
*   **Saturated Vapor:** Frequent use of unnatural, chemically vibrant neon overlays mixed with classical, painterly color theory.
*   **Light as Form:** Using moving lights to create motion blur that feels like a physical smear of paint across the frame.

### 📐 Composition & Texture
*   **Dynamic Distortion:** Subjects are rarely static; they are caught mid-shatter, mid-leap, or heavily distorted through digital post-processing, glass prisms, or physical scanning.
*   **Textural Fluidity:** Merging the texture of high-fashion fabrics (silk, latex) with digital artifacts, pixelation, and glitch aesthetics.
